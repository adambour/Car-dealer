The engine of the new BMW M3 CS combines a 405 kW/550 hp inline six-cylinder engine with an eight-speed M Steptronic gearbox and the intelligent M xDrive all-wheel drive system. These combined elements guarantee the model an acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h in 3.4 seconds and supreme performance, appreciable whatever the seat occupied. The new BMW M3 CS will be produced from March 2023 in a limited edition at the BMW Group plant in Munich, its launch will begin the same month. The most important markets for this new special edition are the United States, Germany, the United Kingdom and Japan.
